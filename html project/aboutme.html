Where Im From

I am from Iowa City, IA. Well more specifically Coralville, IA. I have lived there my entire life. (well minus college) the population is 20,397. This number fluxuates mostly because Iowa city is home to the University of Iowa, So Students move in and out during breaks and when school is in session. This can mean that traffic is somewhat hetic at times because of all the students that live there. Which can make my commute to work when im back home from college pretty tough. I do like living there mostly because it is a city but not a huge city so I dont feel cramped going to, for example, downtown Iowa City.
