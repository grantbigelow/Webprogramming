College

Last year I started my freshman year at Graceland University as a Computer Science Major. Graceland University is a libral arts college with a population of over 1000 students. I was placed on the house Cheville and became an active member. So much so that I took a leadership position as House Chaplain. Graceland has also given me even more oppertunities like being part of the Improv Club, ACM club, CHC president and I got to work at the Graceland University Radio Station. Right now I am finishing up my second year here and I think I chose the right college for me. Graceland is a small university compared to other schools which made class sizes small which is a huge benifit for me. I have also fell in love with the community here.
